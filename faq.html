FAQ

Orders & Shipping

1.How long does it take to process an order?

Most orders are made-to-order and take 5–10 business days to create before shipping.

2.Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship, however for now we only offer in Africa! Please note that delivery times and fees may vary based on location.

3.How will I know when my order has shipped?

You’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your item has been

4.Do you offer delivery locally?

Yes, we offer delivery locally too, you can even come get your order directly from us

Custom Orders

1.Can I request a custom color or size?

Absolutely! We love custom requests. Just contact us with your ideas and we’ll work with you to create something unique.

2.How long do custom orders take?

Custom orders typically take 7–14 business days depending on complexity.

3.Do you accept custom character or licensed designs?

We do not create copyrighted or licensed character designs (e.g., Disney, Marvel) unless the design is original or royalty-free.

Payments & Returns

1.What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, and other secure payment options at checkout.

2.Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Due to the handmade nature of our items, we do not accept returns. However, if there’s an issue with your order, please reach out within 7 days and we’ll do our best to help.

Product Care

1.How do I wash crochet items?

We recommend gentle hand washing with mild detergent and laying flat to dry. Some items may be machine washable on a delicate cycle—check the product description for care details.

2.Are your products safe for babies and children?

Yes! We use soft, baby-safe yarns and secure stitching.

Gifts & Packaging

1.Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer free gift wrapping and can include a handwritten note—just mention it at checkout.

2.Can I send an item directly as a gift?

Yes! Enter the recipient’s address at checkout and let us know if you’d like to include a message.

Contact & Support

1.How can I contact you with questions or requests?

You can email us anytime at [your email] or use our contact form. We usually respond within 24 hours.
