ABOUT US

Hi, I’m Tino, the maker, dreamer, and the hands behind every stitch. Thank you for supporting handmade crafts.

My Story

What began as a game in times of boredom turned into cozy hobby during winters became a passion project—and eventually a small business—dedicated to bringing warmth and charm to you

Mission and Values

At our crochet business, (Tino Crochets) we strive to provide customers with unique, handmade products that bring joy and creativity to their lives. We believe in the power of handmade items to bring people closer together, and we are committed to creating quality items that will last for years to come.

What We Offer

1. We offer different handmade crafts at affordable and reasonable prices all across the globe.

2. We have online services and also sell physically if you are locally based.

3. We are based in Botswana, Gaborone.
